How Company Grouping Works

Parent Companies: Companies that have other companies grouped under them.

Child Companies: Companies grouped under a parent company. They will display as "Child (Parent)".

Display Names: Custom names that override the default company name in displays.

Example: If "Wise" is grouped under "Firstbase", it will display as "Wise (Firstbase)" in inventory listings.
