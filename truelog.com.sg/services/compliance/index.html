Ensuring Compliance Excellence

TrueLog upholds the highest quality, environmental, and health and safety standards, ensuring excellence in customs compliance and service.

Qualified Experts

Whether you are OEM, ISP, IT distributor, IT reseller, system integrator or managed service provider, Truelog can help you deploy your equipment across the globe.

EOR/IOR

Exporter/Importer of Record (EOR/IOR), Truelog has been provided with professional IOR services for IT equipment, and technology hardware and goods in over 80 countries worldwide.

Declaring Agent

Truelog is certified by Singapore customs as declaring agents. Unlike many freight forwarders, we keep this important value add service in-house.

US/Canada-FIOR

We are registered FIOR in the USA and Canada, ensuring compliance with industry standards, regulations, and laws in both countries.

EU EORI

Truelog is registered as FIOR/EORI in EU to deliver your goods to all 28 countries of the European Union.

Global Network

With an excellent network of operators who are available 24 hours a day and seven days a week, we promise on timely and cost-effective delivery of goods to the desired destination.

Customs Brokerage

As a licensed customs agent, Truelog provides timely clearance services and handles shipments for exhibitions, tradeshows, fairs, equipment for testing purposes, and any temporary cargoes without the need for you to have an entity in Singapore.

Cross Trade

Truelog is able to meet your demand for overseas freight forwarding and can arrange to ship your goods from anywhere in the world to your required final destination.
