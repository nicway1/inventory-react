Global Fulfillment Services in Singapore

Continously Moving Forward

We operate multiple warehouses at Changi International Airport in Singapore, primarily focused on handling high-value IT equipment. Located within the Free Trade Zone (FTZ), these facilities are fully managed by our in-house team using our proprietary systems and equipment. Our flagship warehouse is also a testing ground for new services before we roll them out in our oversees warehouses.

Singapore Flagship Warehouse

Operating a warehouse at Changi Airport offers strategic advantages, including access to a major global logistics hub, which facilitates faster and more efficient international shipping. Additionally, being located within the Free Trade Zone (FTZ) provides cost benefits by reducing or eliminating customs duties, making it ideal for handling high-value IT equipment.
