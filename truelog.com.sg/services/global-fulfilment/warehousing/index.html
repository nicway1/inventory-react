What We Offer

Global Warehousing Solutions

At TrueLog Singapore, we pride ourselves on providing comprehensive global warehousing solutions tailored to meet the diverse needs of our clients. Our state-of-the-art WMS (Warehouse Management System) ensures efficient handling and management of inventory, from receipt to delivery, guaranteeing seamless operations and customer satisfaction.

Key Services

1. Receiving and Delivery

Efficient receiving and delivery with prompt AWB handling, clearance coordination, and detailed client receipts.

  • AWB Handling: Upon receiving the Air Waybill (AWB) from our customers, we promptly determine the terminal and coordinate the shipment accordingly. This ensures smooth processing from the terminal to the designated agent.
  • Clearance Coordination: Whether from the terminal or another agent, we manage the clearance process efficiently to ensure timely delivery.
  • Client Receipts: We generate detailed receipts for clients, showing the receiving and delivery dates along with their specific details.

2. Crating & Packing Services

We offer a range of crating and packing services to ensure the safe and secure transport of your goods:

  • Customised Pallet Crates: Tailored to specific shipment requirements, our custom crates provide the perfect fit and maximum protection.
  • Wooden Crates: Durable and sturdy, our wooden crates offer excellent protection for various types of cargo.
  • Plastic Crates: For higher durability and enhanced protection, we use robust plastic crates
  • Protective Layering: We employ protective layers to safeguard goods against damage during transit.
  • Repackaging: Our repackaging services ensure that all items are packed securely to withstand the rigors of transportation.

3. Advanced Tracking

Efficient receiving and delivery with prompt AWB handling, clearance coordination, and detailed client receipts.

  • Real-time Tracking: Our advanced tracking system allows clients to monitor their shipments in real-time, ensuring transparency and peace of mind.

4. Specialised Handling for Battery Cargoes (DG Class 9)

We specialize in handling battery shipments, classified as Dangerous Goods (DG Class 9), adhering to all safety and regulatory standards. Our expertise in this area ensures that battery cargoes are managed with the highest level of care and compliance, minimizing risk and ensuring safe delivery.
