What We Offer

Key Features

TrueLog offers specialized logistics solutions for complex shipments, ensuring safe and timely delivery worldwide.

1. In-House Framework

Our streamlined process allows us to deliver a draft and original certificate of insurance within minutes, ensuring a quick and hassle-free experience for our clients. This rapid turnaround helps in maintaining the continuity of your logistics operations.

2. Comprehensive Coverage

We offer national and international coverage for all types of transportation, including maritime, land, and air, providing protection regardless of the mode of transit. Whether your cargo is moving across the country or across continents, weve got you covered.

3. All-Risk Coverage

Our insurance policies offer all-risk coverage, safeguarding your cargo against a wide range of perils, including theft, damage, and loss. This means you can rest assured knowing that your goods are protected from a variety of potential threats.

4. Door-to-Door Protection

Our insurance hedges provide end-to-end coverage, ensuring that your cargo is protected from the moment it leaves your premises until it reaches its final destination. This comprehensive approach minimizes risk at every stage of the journey.

5. High Coverage Limits

With cargo insurance coverage of up to USD 3 million, you can trust TrueLog to protect your valuable shipments, no matter their size or value. This high limit ensures that even the most valuable shipments are fully covered.

6. Expert Advice

Our team of professionals offers guidance on the selection of insurance types and incoterms, helping you choose the most cost-efficient and effective insurance solutions tailored to your specific needs. Our experts stay up-to-date with industry trends and regulations to provide the best advice.
