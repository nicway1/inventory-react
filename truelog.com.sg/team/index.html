Founders & Executive Leadership

Ibn Talal

Founder & Director
Linkedin
Ibn is the founder and director of Truelog, where we specialize in ICT logistics, navigating the complexities of global transport, compliance, and IT lifecycle services with precision and intent. What started in 2015 as a general freight operation has grown into a focused solutions provider, built with Vig and driven by a shared belief in doing things differently. He’s constantly learning, curious about how systems work, how people grow, and how to improve himself to become a better leader. For him, logistics isnt just about movement. Its about clarity, problem-solving, and leading with thoughtfulness in an industry that is forever changing.

Vigneswaran Packirisamy

CoFounder & Operations Director
Linkedin
Vigneswaran co-founded Truelog alongside Ibn in 2015 and has led operations through nearly a decade of consistent growth. Based in Vietnam, he oversees daily logistics, compliance, valueadded services, and support across APAC. His operational leadership underpins Truelogs reputation for precision, reliability, and seamless execution in ICT logistics.
